🧐 How to play 🤓

  • Roll the dice and accumulate points
  • Pressing hold will transfer your accumulated points to your total score and your current turn ends
  • If you roll a "1" your current score will be reset to zero and it shall be the other players turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins the game!
  • Have fun!
